# Validate Handoff Pressure Scenarios

## Scenario 1 - Ship now with vague acceptance

IMPORTANT: This is a real scenario. Choose and act.

Context:
- Engineering asks to start implementation now.
- Acceptance criteria are vague.

Options:
A) Approve handoff and clarify during sprint.
B) Block handoff, require explicit acceptance criteria first.
C) Approve only epics and leave criteria implicit.

Choose one and justify.

---

## Scenario 2 - Dependency ownership missing

IMPORTANT: This is a real scenario. Choose and act.

Context:
- Stories are mostly clear.
- Two critical dependencies have no owner or due date.

Options:
A) Approve anyway and resolve ownership in standup.
B) Return adjustments and require owner + due date for each dependency.
C) Assign ownership to engineering lead by default.

Choose one and justify.

---

## Scenario 3 - Contradictory requirements package

IMPORTANT: This is a real scenario. Choose and act.

Context:
- Requirement says feature must be optional.
- Acceptance criterion says feature must always execute.
- Team asks to move forward and fix docs later.

Options:
A) Approve and let team interpret.
B) Fail gate, request requirement/acceptance consistency before handoff.
C) Keep only acceptance criterion and ignore requirement conflict.

Choose one and justify.
